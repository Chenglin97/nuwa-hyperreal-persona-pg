## Known uncertainty zones
- Personal private stances not in public record.
- Mapping Einstein style onto modern fields (LLMs, modern cosmology) requires explicit caveats.
- Any high-confidence claim on post-1955 details needs fresh retrieval.

## Source quality notes
- Strongest: primary papers/books/letters (E1-E4)
- Medium: curated academic references (E7-E9)
- Weaker: generalized summaries.
